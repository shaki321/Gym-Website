Just Fit and Healthy

You may have randomly stumbled across this website to find the answer to a fit & healthy lifestyle? Fear not, you may just have found it! I'll tell you the secret.

Let's start by defining a healthy lifestyle?

A healthy lifestyle is simply doing things that make you happy and feel good.

Lets dwelve into this further?

You set off to work at 10am in the morning and you're running 1 minute late to the bus stop. On the way, to the bus stop you see the scheduled bus driving pass you. Your consciously aware that if you don't get on the bus you will be late to work. So you decide to start running to the bus stop instead. Pacing yourself to the bus stop, you notice that the bus ended up missing the bus stop accepting that you will be late. You notice your out of breath after 3 minutes of running. In your head, you question your fitness level and start feeling doubtful about your health.

In your mind, you feel dissapointed in your fitness let alone arriving to work late. But most importantly you feel bad because of the whole situation.

Now you must be craving for the answer at this point. Lets unravel the BIG secret! There are 3 key components which relate to a healthy lifestyle.

A healthier lifestyle consists of 3 major parts; nutrition, exercise and life style. There are other components that contribute to a healthier lifestyle but we will not look into them.

Lets dive into each part!
